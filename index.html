Common Questions

Clear answers for residents and families.

Do you require drug testing?
Yes. To ensure the safety and sobriety of our brotherhood, we conduct regular and random drug/alcohol screening. This is a core part of our accountability structure.
Is there a curfew?
Yes. We maintain a structured curfew to help residents build healthy routines and ensure the house remains quiet and respectful at night. Specific hours are discussed during intake.
What is included in the weekly rate?
Our rates (starting at $165/week) are all-inclusive. This covers all utilities, high-speed Wi-Fi, access to the gym equipment, coffee bar, laundry facilities, and premium bedding. There are no hidden fees.
Who is eligible to join?
We welcome men who have completed a detox or treatment program (30+ days recommended) and are fully committed to a life of sobriety. You must be willing to participate in our community and adhere to house standards.
